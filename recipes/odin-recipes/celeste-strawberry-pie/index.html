Celeste Strawberry Pie

The Celeste Strawberry Pie recipe tastes similar to how it felt beating the game…epic. Amazing. Cathartic. An easy latticed pie crust, paired with a tangy strawberry filling, make for a delicious summer pie very worthy of the classic game.

Ingredients

  • Unsalted butter
  • All purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Strawberries
  • Cornstarch
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough

    2. Mix all the ingredients together until you can form a dough, divide the dough into two discs, and refrigerate. Using a stand mixer makes this step a lot quicker!

  2. Prepare the Strawberry filling

    3. Mix everything together in at-once to create the filling, and set aside so it has a little time to thicken.

  3. Bake the Strawberry pie

    4. This section involves preparing the pie dish, pouring in the pie filling, and creating a lattice on top using more dough. The assembled pie crust is treated with an egg wash before baking. (It’ll need a few hours to cool before being ready to eat too. But that wait is worth it!)
